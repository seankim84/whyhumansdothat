Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This policy explains what information Why Humans Do That ("we," "us") collects when you visit our website or buy a product, and how we use it. We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the store.

Information we collect

  • Purchase information. When you buy a product, our payment provider Polar collects your email address and payment details to process the order and deliver your download. We receive your email and order details, but never your full card number.
  • Communications. If you email us, we receive your email address and the contents of your message.
  • Basic technical data. Our host (Cloudflare) may process standard server and security logs, such as IP address and browser type, to deliver and protect the site.

How we use it

  • To process your purchase and deliver the product.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your messages.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website.

Payments

Payments are processed by Polar as the merchant of record. Your payment is handled under Polar's own privacy and buyer policies. Card details are entered on Polar's secure checkout and are never stored by us.

Third parties

We rely on a small number of service providers to run the store, including Polar (payments and delivery) and Cloudflare (hosting and security). We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by emailing us. Some records may be retained where required for tax, accounting, or legal reasons.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email architect@anchors.work.

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.
